About me

Hi there!

I'm a young web designer based in Milan, from Paris, with a British nationality. I studied communication in Paris first, then went on to studying Web and digital design in Milan.

I love designing user-friendly interfaces, constantly trying to find new ways to do things.

I enjoy as much the designing aspect as I do the development and I believe it all goes hand in hand.

Have a look at my work if you want to, and don't hesitate to contact me for anything!
